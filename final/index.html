Program Info

Learn how Juab Girls Wrestling builds confidence, discipline, and strength in athletes of all ages.

Juab Girls Wrestling Program

Our program is designed for wrestlers of all skill levels — from first-time beginners to experienced competitors.

  • ✔ Builds confidence, discipline, and mental toughness
  • ✔ Encourages teamwork, sportsmanship, and resilience
  • ✔ Teaches fundamental and advanced wrestling techniques
  • ✔ Creates a positive and supportive team culture
  • ✔ Helps athletes prepare for competitive matches and tournaments

Whether your wrestler is just starting or ready to advance, Juab Girls Wrestling provides a place to grow, improve, and succeed.
