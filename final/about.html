About Our Program

About Juab Girls Wrestling

Juab Girls Wrestling is built on hard work, discipline, and a love for the sport. We train athletes from beginner to advanced levels, helping each girl gain confidence, leadership skills, and strength on and off the mat.

Our Mission

To create a positive, competitive, and supportive environment where young female wrestlers can grow, challenge themselves, build confidence, and discover their full potential.

What We Stand For

  • Discipline: Building strong habits through consistent training.
  • Confidence: Helping athletes believe in themselves on and off the mat.
  • Respect: Teaching sportsmanship, teamwork, and integrity.
  • Growth: Every practice, every match—always improving.

Coaching Staff

Our coaching staff is dedicated to teaching fundamentals, advanced technique, and strong sportsmanship. Even without photos, each coach contributes to creating a safe, structured, and motivating environment for all athletes.

KayCee Anderson

KayCee brings patience, positivity, and strong fundamentals to the Little League program. She helps young athletes build confidence while learning the basics of wrestling in a fun, encouraging environment. Her connection with the kids makes practices productive and enjoyable.

Tanner Kowan

Tanner brings energy and structure to the Little League team. He teaches discipline, proper technique, and the importance of hard work—while ensuring every athlete feels supported. His guidance helps young wrestlers develop strong habits early on.

Selony Christensen

Selony leads the Jr High girls with a focus on maturity, growth, and self-belief. She challenges athletes to refine technique, develop mental strength, and rise to their potential both on and off the mat.

Garret Cannon

Garret brings experience, intensity, and deep wrestling knowledge to the Jr High program. He teaches advanced technique while emphasizing discipline, respect, and resilience. His coaching prepares athletes for higher-level competition with confidence.
