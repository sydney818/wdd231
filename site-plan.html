Site Plan

Site Name

Juab Girls Wrestling

It clearly identifies the team and the purpose of the website.

Site Purpose

The website will share information about the Juab Girls Wrestling team, including team details, photos, and general information for parents and athletes.

Common Questions

  • Where can I find information about the coaches?
  • Where can I see team photos and event pictures?

Color Scheme

Gold (#C2B490): Used for accents and section backgrounds.

Burgundy (#7A1C1C): Used for headings and important highlights.

Black (#000000): Used for body text and general readability.

Typography

Montserrat: Used for all headings and body text. Clean and modern, fits well with the team theme.
